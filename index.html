Analysis Summary

The subject property is a 1.55-acre commercial assembly comprising 301 Cumberland St (~2,800 SF Office), 628 Orange St (~1,203 SF Retail), and two vacant parcels. The site is zoned CC (Community Commercial), offering significant redevelopment potential given its low building-to-land ratio. While the existing structures provide immediate utility, the primary value driver is the 0.90-acre surplus tract. A recorded UST incident on the adjacent parcel to the south (Orange St) is noted; however, no on-site environmental issues were identified. The valuation accounts for the current foreclosure status and the contributory value of the vacant land components.
